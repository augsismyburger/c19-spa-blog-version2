Meet Matt Eugene Augsburger

Matt spent his early life in the midwestern U.S., enjoying small town life and the leisure it lends. Matt enrolled at Harding University in Searcy, AR shortly after graduating from Eureka High. Matt was interested in many areas of discipline and struggled to find a major to focus on. Eventually landing on kinesiology, Matt graduated in 2012 and shortly after moved to Nashville, TN. M.E. stayed in Nashville for less than a year before moving back to central Illinois and focusing on his fitness career. M.E. worked for a community center gym for three years before taking a leap of faith and starting his own personal training and group fitness business, appropriately named Augsburger Fitness. Matt found out after less than a year that the entrepenuer life wasn't for him, and missing the social life that Nashville provided, he headed back to the Music City in July of 2016. After weighing his career options and goals, M.E. decided to return to school, and in February of 2017 was accepted to Nashville Software School. Matt can usually be found coding, running, or rock climbing.
